Are there differences in activity patterns between weekdays and weekends? 1. Create a new factor variable in the dataset with two levels – “weekday” and “weekend” indicating whether a given date is a weekday or weekend day.

library(plyr)
tt$Date<-as.POSIXct(strptime(tt$Date,"%Y/%m/%d"))
weekday<-weekdays(tt$Date)
tt<-cbind(tt,weekday)
tt$weekday<-mapvalues(weekday,c("Monday","Tuesday","Wednesday","Thursday","Friday","Saturday","Sunday"),c("weekday","weekday","weekday","weekday","weekday","weekend","weekend"))
